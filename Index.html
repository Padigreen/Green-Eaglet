About OJ MARIA

Welcome to OJ MARIA, home of KILISHI DELIGHT! We're passionate about sharing our love for authentic, spicy, and irresistibly crispy kilishi. Prepared with care, handed down recipes, and the finest ingredients, our kilishi brings you a true taste of tradition, straight from our heart to your home.

Our Kilishi

Kilishi Delight comes in a family pack of 125g and a small pack of 62g!

Kilishi is a thin, spicy, sun-dried beef snack rooted in Northern Nigerian heritage.
Ours is crunchy, spicy, and bursting with flavor – perfect for snacking solo or sharing with friends.
